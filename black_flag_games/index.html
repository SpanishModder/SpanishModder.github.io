Papers, Please

Papers, Please is a puzzle-simulation game by Lucas Pope where you work as a border inspector in the fictional communist country of Arstotzka. Players review documents and decide who may enter while supporting their family under political pressure and moral dilemmas.

Spore

Spore (2008) is a unique, open-ended god game developed by Maxis and designed by Will Wright, allowing players to evolve a species from a microscopic cell to a space-faring civilization. It features five distinct gameplay stages—Cell, Creature, Tribe, Civilization, and Space—combining simulation, strategy, and role-playing elements, all utilizing extensive procedural generation for creativity.

Balatro

Balatro is a popular, highly-rated poker-themed roguelike deck-builder focused on creating powerful synergies using Joker cards to reach high score targets. Players modify a standard deck with Tarot and Planet cards, aiming to break the game's rules through combinations, with 150 unique Jokers providing varied strategic advantages.

Plants vs. Zombies

Plants vs Zombies is a 2009 tower defense video game developed and published by PopCap Games. First released for Windows and macOS, the game has since been ported to consoles, handhelds, and mobile devices. The player takes the role of a homeowner amid a zombie apocalypse.

Age of History II

Age of History II (formerly Age of Civilizations 2) is a simple-to-learn, hard-to-master grand strategy wargame developed by Łukasz Jakowski. Players manage nations through turn-based diplomacy, economy, and expansion across a detailed world map. It features historical scenarios, a map editor, and a focus on grand strategy conquest, featuring high player praise for its accessibility.
